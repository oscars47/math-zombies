Compared with the CNOT-based approach, various controlled unitary operations can be implemented more efficiently with less resources and less operations. We will show that, for the realization of a general (n − 1)-control-1 unitary operation, the required resources, e.g. ancilla coherent states, ancilla single photons, can be reduced from exponential to linear, providing an optimization of such unitary operation. Furthermore, two approaches for realizing a general n-qubit unitary operation are proposed.

The rest of the paper is organized as follows. We firstly present the improved optical realization of the two element gates, c-path and merging gate. These element gates will be used to construct various multi-control gates and the general unitary operation in next part. Afterward, we will discuss the complexity of our approach and compare it with the CNOT-based approach. Then the paper is concluded with the final part.
